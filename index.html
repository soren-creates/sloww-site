The Sun Ring

A golden arc traces your daylight from sunrise to sunset. It stretches through summer and shrinks in winter. Your day's shape, always changing.

Dawn & Dusk

Sunset isn't a switch. It's a slow, beautiful fade. Three twilight bands and golden hour highlights show you the whole transition.

The Moon Ring

An inner ring tracks the moon across your sky. The icon shows tonight's phase: full, crescent, new. Updating as the cycle turns.

Lunar Cycle & Seasons

Delicate inner rings track the 29.5-day lunar cycle and the year's progress. Solstices, equinoxes, and where you are right now.

Box Breathing

The innermost ring guides a calming 16-second cycle. Inhale, hold, exhale, hold. A small moment of stillness, whenever you need it.

Any Place on Earth

Search any city or enter coordinates. Save your favorites and see what daylight looks like on the other side of the world.
